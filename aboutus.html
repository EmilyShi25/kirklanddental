Meet the team:




Dr. Teresa Yagi

Meet Dr. Teresa Yagi, a highly skilled and experienced dentist with over 10 years of practice. She specializes in providing comprehensive dental care for patients of all ages, from regular check-ups and cleanings to more complex procedures such as teeth whitening, fillings, and root canals. Dr. Yagi is known for her gentle touch and warm bedside manner, putting even the most nervous patients at ease. With her state-of-the-art equipment and cutting-edge techniques, she is dedicated to helping her patients achieve and maintain optimal oral health. Whether you need a routine cleaning or a full smile makeover, Dr. Yagi is the dentist for you.

Dr. Lauren Ma

Meet Dr. Lauren Ma, a highly respected and accomplished dentist with over 15 years of experience. She is dedicated to providing the highest quality of dental care to her patients, utilizing the latest technologies and techniques in the field. Dr. Ma's areas of expertise include cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and restorative dentistry. She is known for her gentle and compassionate approach, ensuring that her patients feel comfortable and well-informed throughout their treatment. With her state-of-the-art equipment and attention to detail, Dr. Ma is able to deliver beautiful, long-lasting results that exceed her patients' expectations. Whether you're looking for a simple teeth cleaning or a complete smile makeover, Dr. Ma is the dentist for you.

Laurie

She has been working in dentistry since 1983 and began working at Kirkland Dental in 2014. Her responsibilities include treatment planning, insurance claims, scheduling patients, credentialing, payroll and many other front office duties. She majored in speech communications from Oregon State University in 1991. Her father was a dentist and she worked with him starting at age 15 until he retired in 2013. She loves making a difference in someone's day, and if she can make someone smile, it truly makes her happy.


Jody

She provides chairside assistance to all the doctors during dental procedures, orders supplies, maintains equipment, and helps ensure that the back office runs smoothly. In 1998, she began working part-time as a sterilization technician and made it her full-time career in 2000. She happily joined Kirkland Dental in 2014. Her favorite part of her job is getting to establish relationships with patients and their families. She loves working with the Kirkland Dental team because everyone is very knowledgeable about their work and have fun doing it.


Jen

Jen completed her undergraduate degree at the University of Washington and later the Dental Hygiene program at Shoreline Community College. Dentistry and all its connections to systemic health and quality of life are deeply meaningful to Jen. She has become passionate about empowering patients through understanding of their oral health and endeavor to make it a relaxed, supportive, and maybe even fun or happy experience at the dental office!
