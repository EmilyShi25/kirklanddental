Dr. Teresa Yagi

Meet Dr. Teresa Yagi, a highly experienced and skilled dentist with over 10 years of practice. She specializes in providing comprehensive dental care for patients of all ages, from regular check-ups and cleanings to more complex procedures such as teeth whitening, fillings, and root canals. She is known for her gentle touch and warm bedside manner and uses state-of-the-art equipment and cutting-edge techniques to help patients achieve and maintain optimal oral health.

Dr. Lauren Ma

Meet Dr. Lauren Ma, a highly accomplished dentist with over 15 years of experience. She specializes in cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and restorative dentistry using the latest technologies and techniques. Known for her gentle and compassionate approach, Dr. Ma ensures patients feel comfortable during treatment and delivers beautiful, long-lasting results. She is the dentist for you whether you need a simple teeth cleaning or a complete smile makeover.
